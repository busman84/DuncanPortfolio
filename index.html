About Matthew Live. Love. Laugh.

Matthew was born in El Paso, Texas on September 2, 1984. Growing up he became infatuated with games of all types. From chess to Super Mario Bros. to basketball, there wasn't a game that he wouldn't play. He has taken his love of playing games and turned it into a passion for developing games for the next generation. His passion for game coding began with simpler steps of learning web design and web application coding which has allowed him to create and maintain many beautiful web pages for businesses, individuals, and just for fun! He also loves to perform for people, wether it be stand up comedy, sketch comedy, or improv he loves to make people laugh. Oh, and he's really good at foosball.
